## LaTeX
I use LaTeX too much. My setup varies depending on use. For collaboration, I use [Overleaf](https://www.overleaf.com/). For independent work I typically use the [Texpad](https://www.texpad.com/) IDE, although I'm trying to learn vim. I used to use [TeXShop](https://pages.uoregon.edu/koch/texshop/), the default on my Mac distribution, which worked fine.

I usually LiveTeX notes for science classes which don't already have lecture notes. I find that it's considerably easier than handwriting from the perspective of organization, and it gives you a nice compilation of notes at the end of the semester.

My high score on [TeXnique](https://texnique.xyz/) is 148.
