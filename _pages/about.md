I'm a senior at Palo Alto High School studying linguistics, math, and classics.

My research interests are in information-theoretic linguistic typology and mathematically motivated models of language processing, which you can learn more about on my [research](research) page. In classics, I'm interested in the Sanskrit grammatical tradition as well as comparative mythology. Check out my CV [here](/assets/vitae.pdf), or a list of my relevant coursework [here](/assets/coursework.pdf).

Outside of research, I like to play music (sitar and trumpet), learn and document languages, and do [photography](https://www.instagram.com/neil_rathi/) (occasionally). I also take [notes](projects/notes.html)

My name is roughly pronounced /n̪iːl ɽɑʈʰiː/. The ⟨th⟩ refers to an aspirated retroflex stop /ʈʰ/, rather than a dental fricative /θ/, but you can pronounce it however is easiest. Neil comes from either Sanskrit *नील* (nīl) or Irish *Niall* depending on who you ask, and Rathi is of (as of now) unknown origin.
